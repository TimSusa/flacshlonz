To get FlacShlonz started you have to ensure, that the following libraries are installed (taken from FLAC 1.3.0 and QT 5.2 or higher):

libFLAC++.so.6
libQt5WebKitWidgets.so.5
libQt5MultimediaWidgets.so.5
libQt5Multimedia.so.5
libQt5Widgets.so.5
libQt5Network.so.5
libQt5Sql.so.5
libQt5Gui.so.5
libQt5Core.so.5
libpthread.so.0
libstdc++.so.6
libgcc_s.so.1
libc.so.6
libFLAC.so.8
